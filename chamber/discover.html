  • Timbuktu at a Glance

    • Population: ~70,000 (51% male, 49% female)
    • Median Age: 16.8 years (youthful population)
    • Ethnic Groups: Tuareg, Songhai, Fulani, Moors
    • Key Industries:
      • Agriculture: Corn and rice in the Niger River valley
      • Crafts: Textiles (64%), jewelry (12%), shoemaking (5%)
      • Trade: Historic salt trade via camel caravans
      • Tourism: UNESCO World Heritage sites
    • Historic Legacy: Intellectual and spiritual hub of the 15th century, home to Sankore University
