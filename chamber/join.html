NP Membership

Free of Charge

Designed for non-profit organizations, this membership offers free access to community events and networking opportunities. Share your organization's initiatives with local businesses and build valuable connections.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership

$10/month or $100/year

Ideal for small businesses, this entry-level membership provides a listing in our online directory and discounted event fees. Gain visibility within the local business community while accessing resources to grow your business.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership

$20/month or $200/year

Perfect for growing businesses, this membership includes all Bronze benefits plus priority event placement and one complimentary business spotlight in our monthly newsletter. Boost your visibility and establish your presence in the Timbuktu marketplace.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership

$40/month or $400/year

The ultimate tier for leaders in the community, Gold Membership includes all Silver benefits, premium directory placement, and exclusive access to chamber-hosted VIP events. Elevate your brand and forge high-impact partnerships with local decision-makers.
